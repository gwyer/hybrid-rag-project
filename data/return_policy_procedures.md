# TechVision Electronics Return and Exchange Policy
## Customer Service Procedures Manual

### Version 2.1 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024

---

## 1. Standard Return Policy

### 1.1 Consumer Purchases
- **Return Window:** 30 days from purchase date
- **Condition:** Product must be in original packaging with all accessories
- **Restocking Fee:** None for unopened items; 15% for opened items
- **Refund Method:** Original payment method within 5-7 business days

### 1.2 Business/Commercial Purchases
- **Return Window:** 15 days from delivery date
- **Condition:** Product must be in original packaging
- **Restocking Fee:** 20% for all returns
- **Special Orders:** Non-returnable unless defective

### 1.3 Non-Returnable Items
- Products with visible physical damage (customer caused)
- Products missing serial numbers
- Custom installation products
- Demo/display units sold "as-is"
- Products purchased more than 30 days ago

---

## 2. Exchange Procedures

### 2.1 Same Product Exchange
If a customer wants the same product due to defect:
1. Verify purchase within warranty period
2. Document the defect with photos/video
3. Issue RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) number
4. Ship replacement immediately (cross-ship for premium customers)
5. Customer returns defective unit within 14 days

### 2.2 Different Product Exchange
If customer wants to exchange for a different model:
1. Process as return and new purchase
2. Apply any price difference
3. Standard return conditions apply
4. New warranty period starts on exchange date

## 3. Warranty Claims Process

### 3.1 Warranty Coverage by Product Type

| Product Category | Standard Warranty | Extended Available |
|------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| LCD TVs (32"-50") | 1 year | Up to 3 years |
| LCD TVs (55"-75") | 2 years | Up to 5 years |
| LCD TVs (80"+) | 5 years | Up to 7 years |
| OLED TVs | 3 years | Up to 5 years |
| QLED TVs | 2 years | Up to 5 years |
| Monitors | 2 years | Up to 4 years |
| Projectors | 2 years | Up to 4 years |
| Accessories | 1 year | N/A |
| Commercial Displays | 3 years | Up to 5 years |

### 3.2 Warranty Claim Steps
1. Customer contacts support with proof of purchase
2. Agent creates warranty claim ticket
3. Troubleshooting performed (if applicable)
4. If defect confirmed, determine resolution type:
   - Repair at service center
   - On-site repair (for 75"+ products)
   - Replacement unit
5. Issue RMA and shipping label
6. Track resolution to completion

### 3.3 What's NOT Covered
- Physical damage from mishandling
- Power surge damage (unless surge protector was used)
- Software issues from third-party apps
- Normal wear and tear
- Burn-in on OLED (unless extended warranty purchased)
- Pixel policies (varies by product - see section 4)

---

## 4. Dead Pixel Policy

### 4.1 LCD/QLED TVs
Replacement eligible if:
- 5 or more bright (stuck on) pixels
- 8 or more dark (dead) pixels
- 3 or more pixels in a 1-inch cluster

### 4.2 OLED TVs
Replacement eligible if:
- 3 or more bright pixels
- 5 or more dark pixels
- 2 or more pixels in a 1-inch cluster

### 4.3 Computer Monitors
Replacement eligible if:
- 3 or more bright pixels
- 5 or more dark pixels
- Any pixel defect in center 50% of screen

---

## 5. Damaged in Shipping Claims

### 5.1 Visible Damage at Delivery
1. Customer should refuse delivery OR
2. Accept and document damage immediately
3. Report within 48 hours
4. Provide photos of outer box and product damage
5. File carrier claim and arrange replacement

### 5.2 Concealed Damage
1. Customer reports damage within 5 days of delivery
2. Provide photos of packaging and product
3. Original packaging must be retained
4. Replacement shipped; damaged unit picked up

### 5.3 White Glove Delivery Issues
- Any damage noted at installation is carrier liability
- Replacement scheduled within 48 hours
- Installation rescheduled at no charge

---

## 6. Price Match and Adjustment

### 6.1 Pre-Purchase Price Match
- Match verified prices from major retailers
- Must be identical model number
- Does not apply to: clearance, open-box, or member-only prices

### 6.2 Post-Purchase Price Adjustment
- Within 14 days of purchase
- TechVision price drops only
- Refund difference to original payment method
- Does not apply during promotional periods (Black Friday, etc.)

---

## 7. Special Situations

### 7.1 B2B/Corporate Returns
- Contact dedicated account manager
- Custom RMA process applies
- Volume returns require advance notice
- Disposal options available for end-of-life products

### 7.2 Hospitality/Commercial Installations
- Returns require site inspection
- Must be professionally uninstalled
- Original installer responsible for removal
- Restocking fee waived for defects only

### 7.3 Government/Education
- Follow procurement office procedures
- Extended return windows may apply per contract
- Documentation requirements may vary
