## Consumer Trends Analysis

### Screen Size Preferences
The trend toward larger screens continues to accelerate:

- 32"-43": 28% of market (declining)
- 45"-55": 35% of market (stable)
- 65"-75": 27% of market (growing fastest at +15%)
- 80"+: 10% of market (growing at +22%)

**Implication for TechVision:** Continue expanding our large-format TV lineup, particularly in the 75"+ segment. Our LCD 80" TV Flagship priced at $1499.99 is well-positioned.

### Key Purchase Drivers (Consumer Survey Results)
1. Picture Quality: 89% cite as very important
2. Price: 82% cite as very important
3. Screen Size: 76% cite as very important
4. Smart TV Features: 71% cite as very important
5. Gaming Features: 48% cite as very important
6. Brand Reputation: 45% cite as very important
7. Energy Efficiency: 38% cite as very important

## Technology Trends

### Display Technology Evolution
1. **Mini-LED Backlighting:** Rapidly being adopted in premium LCD TVs for improved contrast
2. **QD-OLED:** Samsung's quantum dot OLED gaining traction in premium segment
3. **8K Resolution:** Still niche (<2% market share) due to limited content
4. **MicroLED:** Emerging technology, currently only in ultra-premium segment
