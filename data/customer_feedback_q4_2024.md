# TechVision Electronics Customer Feedback Report - Q4 2024

## Executive Summary
This report compiles customer feedback collected from October through December 2024 across all product lines. Overall satisfaction rating: 4.2/5.0 with significant improvements in OLED product satisfaction.

---

## LCD TV Product Line Feedback

### LCD 32" TV Basic (TV-LCD-32-001)
**Average Rating: 3.8/5.0** | **Total Reviews: 847**

Positive feedback themes:
- Excellent value for money at the $299.99 price point
- Easy setup process, plug and play functionality
- Good picture quality for the size
- Lightweight and easy to wall mount

Common concerns:
- Sound quality could be better, recommend external speakers
- Limited smart TV app selection
- Remote control feels cheap
- Some reports of backlight bleeding at edges

Customer Quote: "Perfect TV for a guest bedroom. Picture is sharp and the price is unbeatable." - Jennifer M., Texas

- HDR support makes movies pop

## OLED TV Product Line Feedback

### OLED 55" TV Premium (TV-OLED-55-001)
**Average Rating: 4.7/5.0** | **Total Reviews: 412**

Positive feedback themes:
- Perfect blacks, incredible contrast
- Wide viewing angles
- Thin and elegant design
- Excellent color accuracy
- Gaming response time outstanding

Common concerns:
- Price premium over LCD alternatives

### OLED 77" TV Elite (TV-OLED-77-001)
**Average Rating: 4.8/5.0** | **Total Reviews: 89**

Positive feedback themes:
- Cinema-quality picture at home
- Premium build quality justifies $2499.99 price
- Perfect for home theater enthusiasts
- WebOS interface smooth and intuitive
- Dolby Vision and Atmos support excellent

Common concerns:
- Brightness could compete better with LCD in sunny rooms

## Common Themes Across All Products

### Strengths Identified:
1. Competitive pricing across all product categories
2. Good build quality and durability
3. Comprehensive warranty coverage
4. Improving smart TV platform
5. Strong gaming feature set

### Areas for Improvement:
1. Audio quality on built-in TV speakers
2. Smart TV app loading speeds
3. Remote control design and build quality
4. Customer support response times
5. Installation guidance documentation
